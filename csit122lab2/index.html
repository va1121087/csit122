MY Favorite Recipe:

Tuna Fish on Toast

  1. 2 Boiled eggs
  2. Can of Albacore Tuna
  3. Pickled Relish
  4. Lowry's Seasoned Salt
  5. 4 slices of white bread
  6. Pepper
  7. Mayonnaise
  8. Small onion
  9. 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Toast both sides of bread to your liking

  1. Open the tuna, peel the boiled eggs and place into a mixing bowl. Mash boiled eggs into pea-sized pieces and mix with tuna
  2. Dice a soup spoon full of onions and add to the mix.
  3. Add a pinch of Lowry's and pepper as well as two and a half teaspoons of mayo
  4. Proceed to empty the batter into baking pan then, gently insert into the oven
  5. Refrigerate for 45 minutes
  6. Remove from fridge, and IMMEDIATELY spread tuna on toast. Add cheese slices
  7. Let sit for 20 minutes
  8. Eat & ENJOY!!!!!!

This is my favorite recipe because it is healthy, quick, and easy to make. You can always add or subtract ingredients as you see fit.
