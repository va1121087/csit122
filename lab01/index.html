MY Favorite Recipe:

Blue Berry/ Raspberry Cornbread

  1. 2 Brown eggs
  2. 1/2 box of Jiffy Blue Berry Cornbread
  3. 1/2 Box of Jiff Rasbery Cornbread Mix
  4. 1 Box of Jiffy CornBread Mix
  5. 1/2 Cup of Whole Milk
  6. 1 Stick of Butter
  7. 1 Table Spoon of Cinnmamon
  8. 1/2 Table Spoon Vanilla Extract

PREHEAT OVEN TO 400*

h1 { color:red}

  1. Place all items into mixing bowl except the stick of butter

  2. Proceed to mix ingtriedients into it develops a lumps free consisdtency
  3. Butter the baking pan generously on all side to ensure Cornbread doesn't burn/stick to pan
  4. Proceed to empty the batter into baking pan then, gently insert into oven
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until Golden Brown
  6. Remove from oven, and IMMMEDIATELY spread what left of entire stick of butter accorss entire pan of cornbread
  7. Let sit for 20 Minute
  8. Eat & ENJOY!!!!!!

This is my favorite recipe because it has become a staple of my community, feeding many improverished black families for generations. My grandmother will be 98 years old on 90 days. She oftern reminds me of how much things were different in her day when black people in America were not afforded educational/economic opportunity beacuse of racism and poverty and institutional racism. She tells me how little things like food were a big deal , and cornbreadbread was a meal by itself, and Blue/Raspbery Cornbread with butter was every poor black child's favorite treat.
